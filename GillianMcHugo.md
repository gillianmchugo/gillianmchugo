## [Gillian McHugo](https://gillianmchugo.github.io) :cow2::dna::computer:

Computational biology PhD researcher at [University College
Dublin](https://www.ucd.ie) studying integrative genomics of infectious
diseases in cattle, focusing on trypanosomiasis in hybrid African cattle
and bovine tuberculosis in Irish cattle.
